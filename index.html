This website is a space for me to upload some of the projects I'm working on.

It's been a couple of years since I last picked up a maths textbook, so when I first looked at 'Causality' by Pearl, and 'An Introduction to Statistical Learning' I found it more difficult than I would have imagined to really commit what I was learning to memory.

To help with this I'm using two approaches:

  • ANKI - a spaced learning flashcard app
  • Theorem maps - a graph where nodes are theorems, lemma, or definitions, and directional edges show how these are built
